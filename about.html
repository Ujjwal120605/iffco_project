Our Story

Indian Farmers Fertiliser Cooperative Limited (IFFCO) is one of the world's largest fertilizer cooperatives, wholly owned by Indian farmers. Established in 1967, IFFCO was born out of the vision to serve farmers through cooperative efforts and ensure food security for the nation.

Founded with the mission to make fertilizers easily accessible to farmers at affordable prices, IFFCO has grown into a multi-state cooperative society with over 36,000 affiliated cooperative societies and more than 5.5 crore farmer members across India.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To serve farmers through cooperative efforts in the field of production, procurement, and distribution of agricultural inputs including fertilizers, seeds, and other products, thereby contributing to food security and improving the socio-economic conditions of the farming community.

Vision

To be a world-class cooperative organization providing comprehensive solutions for sustainable agriculture, ensuring food and nutritional security while empowering farmers through innovative technology and cooperative principles.

Key Services & Products

Fertilizers

Manufacturing and distribution of high-quality fertilizers including Urea, DAP, NPK, and specialty fertilizers to enhance crop productivity.

Seeds & Pesticides

Premium quality seeds for various crops and effective pesticides to protect crops from pests and diseases.

Digital Services

IFFCO Kisan mobile app and digital platform providing weather updates, crop advisory, and market information.

Farmer Education

Training programs, workshops, and educational initiatives to promote modern agricultural practices and technology adoption.

Renewable Energy

Solar power projects and energy-efficient solutions contributing to sustainable agriculture and environmental conservation.

Cooperative Banking

Financial services through cooperative banks and credit facilities to support farmers' financial needs.

Our Journey

1967
IFFCO was established as a multi-state cooperative society with the vision to serve farmers.
1975
First manufacturing unit established in Kalol, Gujarat for ammonia and urea production.
1985
Expanded operations with new manufacturing facilities and increased production capacity.
1995
Diversification into specialty fertilizers and agricultural chemicals.
2010
Launch of IFFCO Kisan Sanchar Limited for rural telecommunication services.
2020
Digital transformation with IFFCO Kisan mobile app and online services for farmers.
2024
Continued innovation in sustainable agriculture and renewable energy solutions.
